Norbert Wiener

Depression Famously eccentric

Best known as the inventor of Cybernetics - the theoretical foundation which has given us servomechanisms, artificial intelligence and neuroscience.

A famously neurotic individual, Wiener feared for the future based on two fundamental convictions

  • We humans can’t resist selfishly misusing the powers our machines give us, to the detriment of our fellow humans and the planet.
  • +
  • We couldn’t control our machines even if we wanted to, because we're increasingly building them to make decisions on their own.
"The world of the future will be an ever more demanding struggle against the limitations of our intelligence, not a comfortable hammock in which we can lie down to be waited upon by our robot slaves."
